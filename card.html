Florida Panther

The Florida Panther is one of North Americas most endangered animals and has many different names including Puma and Coguar

  • Trinomial name: Puma concolor cougar
  • Speed: 64-80 [km/h]
  • Growth:60-90 [cm]
  • Weight: 53-100 [kg]

Description

Florida panthers are spotted at birth and typically have blue eyes. As the panther grows, the spots fade and the coat becomes completely tan, while the eyes typically take on a yellow hue. The panther's underbelly is a creamy white, and it has black tips on the tail and ears. Florida panthers lack the ability to roar, and instead make distinct sounds that include whistles, chirps, growls, hisses, and purrs.
